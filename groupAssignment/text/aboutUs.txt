Welcome to our program!

Objective:
Our program is dedicated to solving the Target 13.3 in SDG 13.
Its main goal is improve education, awareness-raising, and human and institutional capacity on climate change mitigation, adaptation, impact reduction, and early warning.

Introduction:
Climate change is a global challenge that requires the concerted efforts of people around the world.
Our program aims to provide resources and knowledge to individuals and society,
and to raise people's awareness of climate change in order to actively participate in climate action.
We foster people's sense of responsibility to sustainable practice through educational advocacy and awareness promotion activities.
